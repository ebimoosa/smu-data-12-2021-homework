About the dataset

Belly Button Biodiversity

The data used to create this dashboard came from NC State University. The study was condutcted from 2011 to 2012 and looks at the microbes that grow in the belly button. The study looked to answer the following questions:

What and how many species live in the belly button?

Why are some species common in many belly buttons and (most) others are rare?

What factors determine the number and kinds of microbes we see in individual belly buttons?

Bacteria: Good or Bad?

The bacteria that lives on the surface of your skin can be both good or bad. Bacteria like Bacillus helps fight other harmful pathogens before they can harm us, this includes other bacteria and fungi!
