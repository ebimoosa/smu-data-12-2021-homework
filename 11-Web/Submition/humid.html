Latitude vs Humidity

This graph compares how humidity changes with latitude. The graph shows that there is little to no correlation between latitude and humidity. This is becuase humidity is not just dependent on temperature, but also air pressure. Air pressure is not affected by latitude so the graph shows only a very weak correlation between humidity and latitude.
